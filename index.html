Project Goals

Learn How to Use High Speed Camera and Thermal Camera

Group members learned how to use high speed camera and thermal camera devices. Information required to use these devices obtained from guides of respective devices and personal experience of our group leader, Ata Shaker. We held meetings at different times to understand how we can use these devices, then we decided which objects would provide meaningful records to process later.

Apply Digital Image Processing Methods on Recordings

We discussed which digital image processing methods we can apply on the recordings we obtained to provide meaningful insights. After deciding on the processes, we searched on practiacal tools to realize them. Because of time constraint and complexity of writing very efficient algorithms (especially in the case of high speed recording, very effective algorithms required given the amount of data to be processed) we used pre-built Python library OpenCV to process video recordings.

Prepare Report, GUI and Presentation

After obtaining final form of processed videos a presentation is formed to show our work to our professor and classmates. We chose to design GUI as awebsite, because we thought it would be much more easier to share it and we can create easy to use pages.
