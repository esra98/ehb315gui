Project Goals

Learning How to Operate High-Speed and Thermal Cameras

In this project, the group members learned how to work with high-speed and thermal cameras. The necessary information regarding the cameras was provided by the group leader, Ata Shaker. Throughout the semester, multiple face-to-face/online meetings were held to debate the upcoming recording target and for further organization and cooperation among the team members.

Applying Digital Image Processing Techniques on The Captured Footages

Group members continually engaged in democratic discussions on the types of image processing techniques that would yield the best outcome and provide more meaningful insights. After settling down on the image processing techniques, the members inquired about practical means to realize them. Due to time constraints and the efficiency of the existing algorithms, we used the pre-built library OpenCV on Python to process the recordings.

Preparing Report, GUI, and Presentation

After processing the captured footages up to a satisfactory point, the team as required by the project criteria, prepared a meticulous and comprehensive report, a gorgeous General User Interface in the form of a web application, and finally a presentation.
